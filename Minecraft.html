Minecraft Snapshot 25w45a Leaks Netherite Horse Armour Ahead of Mounts of Mayhem Update

What’s new:

A new snapshot (25w45a) has gone live for the upcoming “Mounts of Mayhem” drop, introducing Netherite horse armour among other features.

Why it matters:

This hints at a major upcoming update. Mounts (rideable entities), stronger armour for them, and potentially new mechanics (mount combat/pets) show the sandbox is evolving. For readers, this builds anticipation.
